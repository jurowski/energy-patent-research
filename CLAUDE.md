# Energy Patent Research

## Purpose
Open-source research project analyzing publicly available energy device patents
to identify common principles, topologies, and materials among devices claiming
high efficiency or unconventional energy conversion methods.

## Goals
- Catalog and analyze public energy patents (USPTO, EPO, WIPO)
- Identify recurring technical patterns across high-efficiency claims
- Synthesize findings into reproducible, open-source designs
- All findings published openly — no patents, no secrets

## Key Directories
- `research/` — patent analysis notes, categorized by technology
- `patterns/` — identified common principles and recurring themes
- `designs/` — open-source device concepts derived from research
- `data/` — structured patent data, CSVs, scraped metadata
- `scripts/` — tools for patent search, analysis, and visualization

## Tech Stack
- Python for patent data scraping/analysis
- Markdown for research notes
- SQLite for patent metadata storage

## Data Sources
- USPTO full-text patent database (public)
- Google Patents (public API)
- EPO Open Patent Services
- WIPO PATENTSCOPE
